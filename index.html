Our Story

A house built
around the ritual
of coffee.

AMBRE began as a single roasting bench in a converted warehouse. Twelve years on, we still roast in small batches, cup every lot before it reaches a cup, and design every room to slow people down — low light, warm wood, and conversation worth lingering over.
